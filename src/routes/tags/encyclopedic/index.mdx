# Encyclopedic content

Please use discretion when consuming encyclopedic content.

Encyclopedic content can contain denser information than tutorial content / content catered and curated for beginners.

Encyclopedic content may include content that may not be necessary in learning the Japanese languages. However, such content may prove to be interesting to a few people.

Among such content may include:

* Historical context
* Technicalities
* Linguistics
